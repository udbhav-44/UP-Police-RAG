Subject: Application under Section 482 CRPC No.43739/2022 Gagiti Prasad and 6 others vs. Uttar Pradesh State and 3 others passed in compliance with the order of the Hon'ble High Court Allahabad dated 13.04.2023 regarding compliance of DG Circular No.31/2021 and 39/2021 in relation to the notice of the victim, his guardians and others.

Dear Sir/Madam,

You are aware that criminal bail application number-46998/2020 Junaid vs. Uttar Pradesh State and others were issued detailed instructions through DG Circular Nos. 31 and 39 of 2021 for compliance by 09.07.2021. The timeline was also determined for this work.

Under Section 482 CRPC, the order passed in the above-mentioned application No.43739/2022 dated 13.04.2023, under which the accused applied under the Offences of the POCSO Act, has been directed by the Hon'ble High Court Allahabad to comply with DG Circular Nos.31 and 39 of 2021 regarding the notice of the victim, his guardians and others:

1. Instant application under Section 482 CRPC has been filed by the applicants challenging charge sheet no.01 of 2022 dated 03.10.2022, State vs. Bhagati Prasad and Others along with summoning order dated 15.11.2022 issued by Additional Sessions Judge Special Judge, POCSO Act, Basti, arising out of Crime No.197 of 2022, under Sections 147, 354 (KHA), 323, 504, 506 IPC and 7/8 Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012, Police Station Sonaha, District Basti.

2. A coordinate bench of this court in the case of Junaid vs. State of U.P. and Another has consumed the need for notice to the victim before absence of specific time period in a statute, referring judgments of the Supreme Court in the case of Regional Provident Fund Commissioner vs. Kt Rolling Mills Pvt.Ltd. and this court's judgment in the case of Ajeet Chaudhary vs. State of U.P., and framing timeline for the different statutory functions and service of notice upon the victim in matters pertaining to Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012.

3. Taking note of the necessity of issuance of notice to the victim in the matters filed under Sections 482 CRPC before this court arising out of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 in Junaid (supra).

4. In view of the aforesaid, the Registrar General of this court is directed to issue necessary circular with respect to the directions at the level of Registry to be followed in applications filed under Sections 482 CRPC arising out of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012, as issued for bail applications in Junaid (supra).

5. Learned Government Advocate shall also adhere to the timeline mentioned in Junaid (supra) with regard to service of notice upon the victim, ensuring timely compliance in the matter.

The order passed in Junaid vs. Uttar Pradesh State and others on 13.04.2023 has been served by the accused under the POCSO Act, which is being served to the victim, his guardian and child welfare committee according to a fixed time limit. The order passed in Bhagati Prasad and 6 others vs. Uttar Pradesh State and 3 others dated 13.04.2023, under which the accused applied under the POCSO Act, has also been served.
