Subject: Regarding the Appointment of Inspector on Circle Rating as Required for the Work of Cyber Cell

Dear Sir/Madam,

As you are aware, DG Circular Number 34/23 dated September 9, 2023, was issued with detailed guidelines at every police station in the state regarding the formation, work, and obligations of the state. In the cyber cell, the manpower was determined to be one Inspector-01, two Sub Inspectors, and two Chief Reserves. The chief constable and constable strength was increased to three.

It is clarified that in the manpower to be appointed in the cyber cell, the inspector refers to the inspector-in-charge of the police station. Police stations where an Inspector Police Station has been appointed in place of an inspector-in-charge will have the concerned Police Commissioner or Senior Superintendent of Police appoint this inspector in their office. This inspector will investigate cyber crimes registered in these police stations. Technical and discretionary cooperation will be done by employees of the cyber cell appointed at the police station concerned.

Sincerely,
(Vijay Kumar)
