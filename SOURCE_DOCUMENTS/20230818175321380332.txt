Subject: Criminal Appeal Number 10708/2023 Usman and 2 Others vs. U.P. State and Others and in the criminal appeal number 10793/2023 Usman and 2 others vs. U.P. State and others.

Dear Sir/Madam,

The above-referenced criminal appeal numbers, 10708/2023 and 10793/2023, were presented to the High Court of Allahabad for consideration on whether action can be taken against the accused on the basis of the same crime under the Uttar Pradesh Gangs Activities (Prevention) Act, 1986.

The decision passed by the High Court on August 4, 2023, determined that action can be taken against the accused on the basis of the same prosecution under the Uttar Pradesh Gangs and Anti-Social Activities (Prevention) Act, 1986. Additionally, criminal appeal numbers 569-570/2022 Shraddha Gupta vs. Uttar Pradesh State and others were accepted by the Supreme Court on April 26, 2022, and action can be taken under the same act.

The above-referenced Supreme Court and High Court decisions are being sent with a directive to study these judicial decisions and make your subordinates aware of these legal provisions. Action should be taken accordingly under the Uttar Pradesh Gangs and Anti-Social Activities (Prevention) Act, 1986 against the accused.
